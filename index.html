ABOUT ME

A creative full-stack web developer and designer passionate about transforming ideas into digital experiences.

I'm Timothy Adedotun Alagbe, a budding self-taught Full-Stack Web Developer and Graphic Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. With a passion for code and a keen eye for design, I specialize in crafting visually appealing, user-friendly web experiences that merge creativity with functionality.

My journey into web development began with curiosity about how websites are structured and function behind the scenes. That curiosity evolved into a solid foundation in front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap) and back-end integration with PHP and MySQL. Alongside development, I bring creative skills in design tools like CorelDraw, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva. I thrive on transforming ideas into digital experiences, ensuring every project is efficiently crafted to meet each client’s goals.

Beyond coding and design, I enjoy making music, photography, drawing, gaming, and playing basketball. Determined to evolve as a developer, I’m commited to continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and innovation—with the goal of making meaningful impact, one project at a time.
